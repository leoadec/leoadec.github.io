Quantum research

More advanced strategies of quantum control and quantum error correction will be necessary to build practical quantum computers. While researching the theory of these tools to overcome noise in quantum systems, it is tempting to have a taste of the study of quantum measurements, and from there to veer into foundations of quantum mechanics. I hopped from one side to the other a few times.
